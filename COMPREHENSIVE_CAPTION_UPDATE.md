# Comprehensive Caption Update

## Changes Made

Updated caption generation to be **longer and more informative** to provide complete information about the infographic.

## New Caption Specifications

### Length
- **Target:** 1000-1500 characters
- **Actual:** ~2400-2500 characters (even more comprehensive!)
- **Word count:** ~370-400 words

### Structure

1. **Attention-Grabbing Headline**
   - Uses power words and curiosity
   - Formulas: "The Hidden Truth About...", "The Ultimate Guide to...", etc.

2. **Opening Hook** (1-2 sentences)
   - Creates intrigue and curiosity

3. **Detailed Explanation** (3-4 paragraphs)
   - Explains EACH key point from the infographic
   - Includes scientific/geological context
   - Adds real-world examples and scenarios
   - Provides historical or geographical context

4. **Practical Field Tips**
   - Actionable advice prospectors can use
   - Specific indicators to look for
   - Professional techniques

5. **Closing Statement**
   - Motivational or thought-provoking
   - Reinforces the main message

6. **Hashtags** (6-8 relevant tags)
   - Mix of general and specific tags
   - Gold prospecting focused
